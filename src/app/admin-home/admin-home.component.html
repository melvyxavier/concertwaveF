About Concerts

The History of Concerts

From orchestras and bands to pop singers, symphonies, and more, concerts have been providing folks with musical entertainment for many, many years. In fact, the first concerts began to pop up in the 17th century, with many of these musical performances happening in colleges and even small homes around the world. Early musicians included the likes of Mozart and Beethoven. Today, concerts range in all types of genres, from hip-hop and country to jazz, folk, and more. Not only that, but concerts have grown to include many different venues. Artists may perform at smaller, intimate clubs, huge outdoor arenas, stadiums, and more.


The Live Concert Experience

Live concerts have certainly evolved throughout the years. Back in the 17th century, musical performances were much more informal and cost little, if anything, to attend. Today, concerts are quite the events! Depending on the artist, you may be among swarms of other fans in the crowd, dancing and bopping to the beat. Some shows are more informal, with outdoor standing-room only, while symphonies and orchestras may be held in more traditional venues with assigned seating. The experience truly will depend on the musician(s) you’re there to see! You may let your hair down and dance the night away or get ready to dress up and enjoy a nice evening out.


Notable Performers

Today, there are so many incredible live performers– from those who sing to those who play instruments and, of course, those who do it all! Notable performers include Beyonce, Justin Timberlake, Ariana Grande, Tim McGraw, and many, many more. Of course, popular artists from days past include Prince, Michael Jackson, and Whitney Houston. With the growth of technology and the music industry overall, there are tons of up-and-coming musicians, including those who find fame via the internet. One of the most well-known YouTube success stories is that of Justin Bieber. No matter what genre you enjoy, there is someone who is ready to put on a show for you!
